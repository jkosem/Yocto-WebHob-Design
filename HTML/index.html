l Community

The Yocto Project welcomes contributions from all types of people. You can contribute code, submit patches, file bugs, answer questions on our mailing lists and IRC channels, review and edit our documentation and much more!

The Yocto community includes platform developers, equipment and device manufacturers and other embedded Linux professionals and enthusiasts. In short, people who are doing interesting work on Yocto. Our governance page has more information about the Yocto Project structure and how you can contribute.
